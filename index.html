The Quantum Era Transitions from Theory to Experimentation

2025 marks a year of accelerated hardware development and standardized security.

Hardware Snapshot (2025)

  • IBM Condor: 1,121 physical qubits (2023)
  • Google Willow: 105 qubits focused on error correction
  • Commercial Access: 100–400 physical/logical qubits via major providers
  • PsiQuantum: Targets 1M physical qubits by 2027–2029

Security & Pilots

  • NIST PQC Standards: ML-KEM, Dilithium, Falcon finalized in 2024
  • Financial Pilots: Goldman Sachs, HSBC piloting quantum portfolio optimization
  • Quantum Advantage: Experimentally demonstrated in risk modeling
